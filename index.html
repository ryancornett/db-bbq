About Us

Danny Boone's BBQ (named after the beloved Reuben Daniel Cornett) got its start indirectly in 2011 when Daniel got the urge to learn how to cook a rack of ribs low and slow. That first rack was not cooked adequately low or slow, and it was a disaster. Commited to discovering how to do it right, Daniel read everything he could find about smoking meat and tried again with a pork shoulder. Over the years, other meats were added to the skillset, the rubs and sauces were perfected, and many who sampled Daniel's fare urged him to sell his smoked delicacies. Thus, Danny Boone's was born.

DB's BBQ uses pork, chicken, and beef from area farmers; seasonal vegetables from regional producers; and locally-sourced, sustainably-harvested maple, hickory, oak, pecan, black cherry, and apple woods to bring our pride to your plate. So come on by and pick up a plate for yourself or a platter for the family. There are several picnic tables beside our lot or in the nearby city park if you'd like to slow down and enjoy our fare outside.
